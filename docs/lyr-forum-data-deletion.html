Lyr Forum — Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 2026

These instructions apply to Lyr Forum at lyr.app when you sign in with Facebook, Google, or a local forum account.

Delete your forum account

  1. Sign in at https://lyr.app/forum/
  2. Open your profile (top-right avatar) → Settings
  3. Choose Delete account and confirm

Request deletion by email

If you cannot sign in, email zeki@lyryq.com from the address linked to your forum or Facebook/Google account. Include your forum username. We delete associated account data within 30 days and confirm by email.

Remove Facebook access

In Facebook: Settings → Apps and websites → remove Lyr Forum. Email us if you also want forum posts and profile data removed.
